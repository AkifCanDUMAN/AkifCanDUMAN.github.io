Arcane

  • Arcane (onscreen titled Arcane: League of Legends) is a 2021 French-American animated television series.
  • Described as an "event series", it takes place in the League of Legends universe.
  • The series was announced at the League of Legends 10th anniversary celebrations.
  • It was produced by Riot Games and Fortiche, a Paris-based French studio that provides animation services. Set in the past, the film retells the origin stories of several characters from Piltover and Zaun.
  • Like the game it's based on, Arcane is aimed at a "16+" audience and will be a little more adult-oriented.
